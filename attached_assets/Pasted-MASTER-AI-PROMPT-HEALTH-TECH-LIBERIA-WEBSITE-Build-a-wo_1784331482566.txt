ABOUT HEALTH TECH LIBERIA

Health Tech Liberia is a non-profit organization committed to reshaping the future of healthcare in Liberia through technology, research, innovation, and advocacy. By integrating digital solutions, promoting gender equity in STEM, strengthening research capacity, and addressing systemic healthcare challenges, Health Tech Liberia is creating a more accessible, equitable, and data-driven healthcare system that improves the lives of communities across Liberia.


---

MISSION

To revolutionize healthcare delivery in Liberia by leveraging technology, research, and innovation to enhance access, efficiency, and quality of care.


---

VISION

To create a future where every individual has access to cutting-edge healthcare solutions, research-driven policies, and sustainable health financing, ensuring healthier communities and a resilient health system.
